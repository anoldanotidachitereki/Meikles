Why Us

Our GOAL, at MEIKLES FOUNDATION is to creat a sustainable ecosystem where the vulnerable and disadvantages in Zimbambwe are empowered


To care for and protect those who are most in need, often children, the youth and the elderly, with a special focus on providing sponsorship and learning for all youth and women in Zimbabwe .To promote sustainable development through initiative protecting wildlife and the enviroment .To creat an


interactive learning and professional enviroment where local business can come together.To protect local artists and contribute to artistic excellence by funding initiative that promote achievements in the literary, visual and performing arts, and to encourage the people of Zimbabwe to appreciate such excellence
